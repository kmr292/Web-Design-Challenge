Wind Speed


Latitude and wind speed have no evident relationship according to the graph above. The data points congregate at the bottom of the graph, indicating that wind speed is usually between 0-15mph regardless of the latitude. However, at latitudes of 60-80 the range of wind speed does appear to be greater, with wind speeds reaching a maximum of 35 mph.
