Humidity


Saturation occurs in air that holds very little water vapor even when the air is relatively dry. Therefore, it is expected that the relative humidity is higher at polar regions. According to the graph, humidity tends to be fairly high regardless of latitude, with the majority of the data points congregating between 60-100% humidity.
